  <h1>Portland Cement Type 1</h1>
  <p>Portland Cement Type 1 is the industry standard for general-purpose construction. Produced by grinding high-quality clinker with a controlled amount of gypsum, it provides a consistent balance of strength and setting time, making it the most reliable choice for everyday building requirements.</p>

  <h2>Cement Strength Classes (kg/cm²)</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>1-225:</b> Light, non-structural tasks or temporary installations.</li>
    <li><b>1-325:</b> Standard grade for masonry and residential construction.</li>
    <li><b>1-425:</b> Widely used for high-quality structural and reinforced concrete.</li>
    <li><b>1-525:</b> Premium grade for heavy-duty infrastructure and high-load elements.</li>
    <li><b>1-625:</b> Specialized ultra-high-strength for pre-stressed concrete.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Key Advantages & Applications</h2>
  <p><b>Advantages:</b> High versatility, consistent strength, excellent workability, and cost-effective.</p>
  <p><b>Applications:</b> Residential/commercial buildings (foundations, columns, slabs), infrastructure (bridges, tunnels, highways), pavements, and precast products.</p>
