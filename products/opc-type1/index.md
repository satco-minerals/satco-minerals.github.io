  <h1>Portland Cement Type 1: The Foundation of Modern Construction</h1>
  <p>Portland Cement Type 1 is the industry standard for general-purpose construction. It is a high-quality hydraulic cement produced by grinding clinker with a controlled amount of gypsum. Known for its versatility, it provides a consistent balance of strength and setting time, making it the most reliable choice for everyday building requirements.</p>

  <h2>Cement Strength Classes</h2>
  <p>While 325, 425, and 525 are the most common grades, Type 1 Portland cement is manufactured across a broader spectrum to meet specific engineering requirements. These classes represent the minimum compressive strength in kilograms per square centimeter (kg/cm²) at 28 days:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>1-225:</b> Used for very light, non-structural tasks or temporary site installations.</li>
    <li><b>1-325:</b> Standard grade for general masonry and residential construction.</li>
    <li><b>1-425:</b> The most widely used grade for high-quality structural and reinforced concrete.</li>
    <li><b>1-525:</b> Premium grade for heavy-duty infrastructure and high-load-bearing elements.</li>
    <li><b>1-625:</b> Specialized, ultra-high-strength grade for pre-stressed concrete and extreme engineering demands.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>High Versatility:</b> Suitable for a wide range of concrete structures.</li>
    <li><b>Consistent Strength:</b> Provides predictable performance and structural integrity.</li>
    <li><b>Workability:</b> Offers excellent handling characteristics during mixing, placing, and finishing.</li>
    <li><b>Cost-Effective:</b> The most economical solution for general building projects.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Residential & Commercial Buildings:</b> Used for foundations, columns, beams, and slabs.</li>
    <li><b>Infrastructure Projects:</b> Ideal for bridges, tunnels, and highways where high-sulfate resistance is not a primary constraint.</li>
    <li><b>Pavement & Walkways:</b> Perfect for sidewalks, driveways, and parking areas.</li>
    <li><b>Precast Concrete:</b> Widely used in the manufacturing of concrete blocks, pipes, and architectural panels.</li>
  </ul>
