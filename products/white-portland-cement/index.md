  <h1>White Portland Cement</h1>
  <p>White Portland Cement is a high-purity binding agent designed for architectural and decorative applications where aesthetic finish is paramount. Produced from raw materials with low iron and manganese content, it delivers a brilliant white color while maintaining the same structural strength and performance characteristics as standard Portland cement.</p>

  <h2>White Portland Cement Strength Classes</h2>
  <p>White Portland cement is produced by using raw materials with very low iron and manganese content to achieve its signature aesthetic color. Beyond its decorative appeal, it is engineered to meet structural requirements and is available in distinct strength classes based on compressive strength in kilograms per square centimeter ($kg/cm^2$) at 28 days.</p>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Aesthetic Appeal:</b> Provides a clean, bright white finish for high-end architectural projects.</li>
    <li><b>Color Versatility:</b> Excellent base for use with pigments to create vibrant, consistent colored mortars and concretes.</li>
    <li><b>High Performance:</b> Combines visual brilliance with robust structural properties.</li>
    <li><b>Uniformity:</b> Offers consistent color and texture across all batches for architectural consistency.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Architectural Concrete:</b> Used for building facades, decorative panels, and artistic structural elements.</li>
    <li><b>Terrazzo & Mosaics:</b> Ideal for premium flooring and decorative surface finishes.</li>
    <li><b>Colored Mortars & Grouts:</b> Perfect for creating customized colored masonry joints and artistic applications.</li>
    <li><b>Precast Decorative Elements:</b> Suitable for statues, balustrades, and intricate architectural castings.</li>
  </ul>
