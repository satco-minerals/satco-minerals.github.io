  <h1>Feldspar</h1>
  <p>Feldspar is a critical industrial mineral group, primarily used as a fluxing agent in ceramic, glass, and filler applications. Our high-quality Potassium Feldspar is processed to provide consistent chemical properties, offering excellent thermal stability and vitrification characteristics essential for high-performance industrial manufacturing.</p>

  <h2>Key Features & Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Superior Fluxing Properties:</b> Effectively lowers the melting temperature in ceramic and glass production, optimizing energy efficiency and surface finish.</li>
    <li><b>Controlled Chemical Composition:</b> High K₂O content ensures reliability and consistency in various industrial formulations.</li>
    <li><b>Low Impurity Levels:</b> Minimal iron content (Fe₂O₃) is maintained to ensure superior clarity and whiteness in end-products.</li>
    <li><b>Optimized Mineralogy:</b> Provides exceptional stability and durability in glazed ceramics and industrial glass formulations.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Ceramic Industry:</b> Utilized as a primary flux in body compositions and glazes to improve strength, hardness, and vitrification.</li>
    <li><b>Glass Manufacturing:</b> Acts as an essential ingredient to increase durability and chemical resistance of glass products.</li>
    <li><b>Filler Applications:</b> Used in the paint, plastic, and rubber industries to enhance structural integrity and chemical inertness.</li>
    <li><b>Architectural Products:</b> Ideal for the production of high-quality porcelain tiles, sanitary ware, and tableware.</li>
  </ul>
