  <h1>SR Clinker (Sulfate-Resistant)</h1>
  <p>SR Clinker is specifically engineered for structures exposed to aggressive chemical environments. By strictly controlling and minimizing the Tricalcium Aluminate (C₃A) content, it provides superior resistance against sulfate attacks.</p>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Chemical Durability:</b> Prevents structural degradation caused by sulfate-rich soil or groundwater.</li>
    <li><b>Low Permeability:</b> Enhances the density of the concrete matrix against harmful ionic penetration.</li>
    <li><b>Long-term Integrity:</b> Ensures the longevity of infrastructure in harsh geological environments.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Hydraulic Infrastructure:</b> Marine structures, sea walls, and harbor projects.</li>
    <li><b>Subterranean Works:</b> Foundations and tunnels exposed to sulfate-rich groundwater.</li>
    <li><b>Industrial Facilities:</b> Sewage systems and chemical processing plants.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Classification & Standards</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>ASTM Standard:</b> Compliant with ASTM C150 Type II/V specifications.</li>
    <li><b>Quality Class:</b> Specialized Industrial Grade.</li>
    <li><b>Chemical Profile:</b> Restricted Tricalcium Aluminate content for sulfate resistance.</li>
  </ul>
