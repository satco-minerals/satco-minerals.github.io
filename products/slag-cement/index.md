  <h1>Slag Cement</h1>
  <p>This cement is manufactured in accordance with the ASTM C595 standard. Due to the incorporation of active silica within the slag, it demonstrates exceptional performance in chemically aggressive environments by minimizing porosity and reducing concrete permeability through continuous secondary hydration reactions.</p>

  <h2>Key Technical Benefits</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Superior Chemical Resistance:</b> Provides excellent protection against the combined attack of sulfate and chloride ions.</li>
    <li><b>Enhanced Durability:</b> Protects steel reinforcement from corrosion and oxidation by reducing permeability and effectively passivating chloride and sulfate ions.</li>
    <li><b>Low Heat of Hydration:</b> Minimizes internal temperature spikes, making it ideal for massive concrete structures.</li>
    <li><b>Improved Workability:</b> Offers superior workability during placement, attributed to the absorption of calcium hydroxide by active silica and the smooth surface texture of the slag particles.</li>
    <li><b>Controlled Setting:</b> Features longer setting times in the short term, resulting in lower expansion levels (near-zero) and high dimensional stability.</li>
  </ul>
