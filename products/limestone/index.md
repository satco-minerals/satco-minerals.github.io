  <h1>Limestone</h1>
  <p>Limestone is a sedimentary rock composed primarily of calcium carbonate (CaCO₃), widely valued for its purity and versatility in industrial, construction, and chemical applications. Our premium-grade limestone, including varieties such as Anguran Limestone, is processed to meet rigorous quality standards, ensuring high whiteness and consistent chemical properties.</p>

  <h2>Key Features & Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>High Purity:</b> Our limestone exhibits high concentrations of calcium carbonate, ensuring superior performance in industrial processing.</li>
    <li><b>Exceptional Whiteness:</b> Specially processed for high brightness and whiteness values, making it ideal for high-end decorative and architectural applications.</li>
    <li><b>Controlled Chemical Composition:</b> Minimized impurities (such as iron and silica) ensure reliability in chemical reactions and manufacturing processes.</li>
    <li><b>Versatility:</b> Suitable for a wide range of uses, including construction aggregates, filler material, and industrial chemical feedstock.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Construction & Architecture:</b> Used as dimension stone, facade material, and high-quality construction aggregate.</li>
    <li><b>Industrial Manufacturing:</b> Essential raw material for cement production, glass manufacturing, and steel refining.</li>
    <li><b>Chemical & Environmental:</b> Utilized for water treatment, flue gas desulfurization, and as a filler in paper, plastics, and paint industries.</li>
    <li><b>Decorative Uses:</b> Preferred in high-whiteness applications for artistic and architectural finishing.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Packaging & Quality Compliance</h2>
  <p>At SATCO, we provide flexible packaging solutions designed to meet the logistical requirements of any construction site or export destination, ensuring product integrity during transport. <b>Supply Mode:</b> Available in bulk (loose) for efficient high-volume transportation and industrial handling.</p>
  <p><b>Standard:</b> Conforms to ASTM C119 (Standard Terminology for Dimension Stone) and ASTM C568 (Standard Specification for Dimension Limestone).</p>
