  <h1>Gypsum Mineral (Raw Material)</h1>
  <p>Gypsum is a vital industrial mineral widely utilized as a setting retarder in cement manufacturing and as a primary component in the production of high-quality construction materials. Our gypsum is sourced and processed to ensure high purity and consistency, meeting the stringent requirements of modern industrial projects.</p>

  <h2>Key Features & Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>High Purity:</b> Essential for maintaining consistent chemical reactions in cement production and plaster board manufacturing.</li>
    <li><b>Optimized Processing:</b> Granulometry controlled at 100% through a 38 mm mesh to ensure uniform distribution and ease of processing.</li>
    <li><b>Low Impurity Levels:</b> Stringent control over chlorine and moisture content ensures the integrity of the end-product and prevents downstream structural degradation.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Cement Industry:</b> Used as a setting time regulator in Portland cement production.</li>
    <li><b>Construction Materials:</b> Base material for drywall, plaster, and architectural finishes.</li>
    <li><b>Soil Amendment:</b> Used in agriculture to improve soil structure and calcium levels.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Packaging</h2>
  <p>At SATCO, we provide flexible packaging solutions designed to meet the logistical requirements of any construction site or export destination, ensuring product integrity during transport.</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Supply Mode:</b> Available in bulk (loose) for efficient high-volume transportation and industrial handling.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Quality Compliance & Delivery Policy</h2>
  <p>To maintain our standard of excellence, all shipments are subject to strict quality verification. Failure to meet these specifications will result in penalties or rejection:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Purity Penalties:</b> Price adjustments are applied for purity levels between 80.0% and 89.9%. Materials with purity < 80.0% will not be accepted.</li>
    <li><b>Moisture & Chlorine Thresholds:</b> Penalties apply for moisture exceeding 3% or chlorine exceeding 0.3%. Loads exceeding 5% humidity or 0.8% chlorine are strictly rejected.</li>
    <li><b>Removal Policy:</b> Any material deemed out of specification must be removed by the supplier within 8 calendar days of notification; otherwise, the contractor will handle the removal at the supplier's expense.</li>
  </ul>
