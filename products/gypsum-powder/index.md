# Gypsum Powder (Plaster of Paris)

Premium grade calcined gypsum, essential for construction finishing works and specialized casting applications. Known for its quick setting time and exceptional workability.

## Key Features
* **Controlled Setting Time:** Optimized for initial and final setting requirements.
* **Fine Particle Size:** Ensures a smooth, high-quality surface finish.
* **High Whiteness:** Derived from pure raw material for aesthetic consistency.
* **Thermal/Acoustic Insulation:** Acts as a natural insulator for interior spaces.
