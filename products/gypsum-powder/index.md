# Gypsum Powder (Plaster of Paris)

Gypsum powder, produced through the calcination of high-quality gypsum rock, is a fundamental material in the construction and manufacturing industries. Known for its quick setting time and exceptional workability, it is essential for finishing works and specialized casting applications.

## Key Properties

* **Controlled Setting Time:** Optimized for initial and final setting requirements.
* **Fine Particle Size:** Ensures a smooth, high-quality surface finish.
* **High Whiteness:** Derived from pure raw material for aesthetic consistency.
* **Thermal/Acoustic Insulation:** Acts as a natural insulator for interior spaces.

## Advantages & Disadvantages

* **Advantages:** Superior adhesion to substrates, high plasticity for decorative details, and rapid drying.
* **Disadvantages:** Highly sensitive to humidity; requires moisture-proof storage; lower structural load-bearing capacity compared to cement.

## Applications

* **Construction:** Interior wall plastering, ceiling finishes, and decorative moldings.
* **Casting:** Production of precise molds for ceramics and pottery.
* **Medical:** Used in the production of medical-grade orthopedic casts.
* **Industrial:** Filler in various specialized chemical and artistic applications.

## Packaging

* **Standard:** Supplied in 25kg or 50kg Paper/PP Bags to ensure protection against moisture and provide ease of handling.

## Quality Compliance & Standards
Complies with ASTM C28 (Standard Specification for Gypsum Plasters) and ISO 15557.
