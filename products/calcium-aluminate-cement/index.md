  <h1>Calcium Aluminate Cement (Refractory Cement)</h1>
  <p>Calcium Aluminate Cement (CAC) is the industry standard for specialized high-temperature and refractory applications. It is a premium hydraulic cement produced by fusing or sintering a controlled mixture of bauxite (alumina source) and limestone (lime source). Renowned for its exceptional thermal resistance, it provides rapid strength development and structural integrity under extreme conditions, making it the most reliable choice for heavy-duty industrial requirements.</p>

  <h2>Cement Refractoriness Classes</h2>
  <p>Calcium Aluminate Cement is formulated across a targeted spectrum to meet specific thermal and chemical engineering demands. These classes represent the maximum service temperature and specialized performance thresholds:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>CAC-40:</b> Standard grade for general refractory castables and chemical-resistant construction.</li>
    <li><b>CAC-50:</b> Premium intermediate grade for iron, steel, and foundry applications requiring balanced thermal stability.</li>
    <li><b>CAC-70:</b> High-purity grade for low-cement castables and severe thermal shock environments.</li>
    <li><b>CAC-80:</b> Ultra-high purity, iron-free grade for critical space-age, petro-chemical, and extreme engineering demands.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Extreme Heat Resistance:</b> Maintains structural strength under continuous operating temperatures.</li>
    <li><b>Ultra-Rapid Hardening:</b> Achieves high mechanical strength within 6 to 24 hours, minimizing industrial downtime.</li>
    <li><b>Chemical & Acid Resistance:</b> Highly resistant to biogenic sulfuric acid corrosion, sulfates, and aggressive chemical environments.</li>
    <li><b>Thermal Shock Defiance:</b> Resists cracking and degradation during rapid temperature cycling.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Industrial Furnaces & Kilns:</b> Used for monolithic linings, arches, and structural walls in steel, cement, and glass factories.</li>
    <li><b>Foundry & Metallurgy:</b> Ideal for casting ladles, troughs, and runners handling molten metals.</li>
    <li><b>Wastewater Infrastructure:</b> Perfect for sewage networks, industrial pipes, and chemical processing plants exposed to aggressive effluents.</li>
    <li><b>Rapid Repair Formulations:</b> Widely used in the manufacturing of rapid-setting repair mortars and technical dry-mixes.</li>
  </ul>
