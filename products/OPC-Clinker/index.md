  <h1>OPC Clinker (Ordinary Portland Clinker)</h1>
  <p>OPC Clinker is the fundamental industrial binder base, manufactured through the precise calcination of raw materials in a rotary kiln. It provides the essential mineralogical balance required for general-purpose construction applications.</p>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Consistent Strength:</b> Reliable mechanical performance at standard 3, 7, and 28-day intervals.</li>
    <li><b>Versatile Compatibility:</b> Fully compatible with standard concrete admixtures for various site conditions.</li>
    <li><b>Standardized Quality:</b> Engineered to meet consistent chemical and physical requirements for global construction projects.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>General Construction:</b> Residential and commercial buildings, bridges, and infrastructure.</li>
    <li><b>Concrete Production:</b> Pre-mix concrete plants requiring standard performance.</li>
    <li><b>General Infrastructure:</b> Pavements, foundations, and urban structural elements.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Classification & Standards</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>ASTM Standard:</b> Compliant with ASTM C150 Type I/II specifications.</li>
    <li><b>Quality Class:</b> Premium Industrial Grade.</li>
  </ul>
