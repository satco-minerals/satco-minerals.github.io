  <h1>Portland-Pozzolan Cement (PPC)</h1>
  <p>Portland-Pozzolan Cement is a blended hydraulic cement produced by inter-grinding Portland cement clinker with high-quality pozzolanic materials. This formulation is engineered to enhance long-term durability, reduce permeability, and improve resistance to chemical attacks, making it an excellent choice for environmental sustainability and aggressive site conditions.</p>

  <h2>Cement Strength Classes</h2>
  <p>These classes represent the minimum compressive strength in kilograms per square centimeter (kg/cm²) at 28 days, ensuring performance in both standard and specialized applications:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>P-325:</b> Used for general masonry, residential construction, and non-structural elements where moderate strength and enhanced durability are preferred.</li>
    <li><b>P-425:</b> The standard structural grade, widely used for foundations, marine structures, and reinforced concrete in aggressive environments.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Enhanced Chemical Resistance:</b> Superior performance in sulfate-rich environments and against chloride penetration.</li>
    <li><b>Reduced Permeability:</b> Creates a denser concrete matrix, preventing the ingress of harmful agents.</li>
    <li><b>Controlled Heat of Hydration:</b> Minimizes the risk of thermal cracking in massive structures.</li>
    <li><b>Environmental Sustainability:</b> Utilizes industrial by-products, significantly reducing the carbon footprint compared to standard cement.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Mass Concrete Structures:</b> Ideal for dams, bridge piers, and foundations where thermal management is vital.</li>
    <li><b>Marine & Coastal Construction:</b> Provides high resistance to salt-water erosion and sulfate attack.</li>
    <li><b>General Structural Concrete:</b> Suitable for columns, slabs, and walls in residential and industrial projects.</li>
    <li><b>Aggressive Soil Conditions:</b> Recommended for foundations in regions with high sulfate or acidic groundwater.</li>
  </ul>
