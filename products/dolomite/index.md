  <h1>Dolomite</h1>
  <p>Dolomite is a naturally occurring carbonate mineral, chemically identified as calcium magnesium carbonate (CaMg(CO₃)₂). Our high-purity Dolomite is widely recognized for its consistent chemical composition and structural integrity, making it an essential raw material for heavy industry and advanced construction applications.</p>

  <h2>Key Features & Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Optimized Chemical Balance:</b> Features a balanced ratio of calcium and magnesium, essential for industrial fluxing and chemical synthesis.</li>
    <li><b>High Purity:</b> Exhibits extremely low levels of impurities, including iron and silica, ensuring superior product performance.</li>
    <li><b>Consistent Quality:</b> Produced with strict adherence to industrial standards, providing reliable results in manufacturing processes.</li>
    <li><b>Granular Options:</b> Available in specific processed sizes of 0-20 mm, 20-40 mm, and 40-80 mm to meet diverse mechanical requirements.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Metallurgical Industry:</b> Serves as a vital fluxing agent in steelmaking and iron production, helping to remove impurities.</li>
    <li><b>Refractory Production:</b> Utilized in the manufacturing of refractory bricks and linings due to its high thermal stability.</li>
    <li><b>Glass and Ceramics:</b> Acts as a stabilizing agent to improve the chemical durability and workability of glass and ceramic bodies.</li>
    <li><b>Construction & Environmental:</b> Used in soil conditioning, water treatment, and as a filler material in industrial coatings and plastics.</li>
  </ul>
