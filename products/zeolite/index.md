  <h1>Zeolite (Natural Mineral)</h1>
  <p>Zeolites are naturally occurring, porous aluminosilicate minerals renowned for their exceptional ion-exchange capacity, molecular sieving properties, and catalytic activity. Often referred to as "molecular sieves," these versatile minerals are essential across a diverse range of industrial, agricultural, and environmental applications, providing sustainable solutions for complex process challenges.</p>

  <h2>Key Features & Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>High Ion-Exchange Capacity:</b> Exceptional ability to adsorb and exchange cations, making them superior for water purification and environmental remediation.</li>
    <li><b>Molecular Sieving:</b> Precise pore structures allow for the selective adsorption and separation of molecules based on size and shape.</li>
    <li><b>Thermal & Chemical Stability:</b> Durable construction resistant to radiation and extreme chemical conditions, ensuring longevity in demanding environments.</li>
    <li><b>Hygroscopic Properties:</b> High capacity for moisture absorption and controlled release, critical for agricultural and energy storage applications.</li>
    <li><b>Catalytic Activity:</b> Adjustable acidity and well-defined pore structures make them highly effective catalysts in petrochemical and industrial reactions.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Agriculture & Horticulture:</b> Acts as a water moderator, slow-release fertilizer carrier, and odor controller. Enhances soil fertility and improves animal health when used as a feed additive.</li>
    <li><b>Environmental Remediation:</b> Highly effective in heavy metal adsorption, wastewater treatment, and the removal of ammonia from aquatic systems and sludge.</li>
    <li><b>Industrial & Petrochemical:</b> Serves as a critical catalyst in fluid catalytic cracking and hydrocracking. Used in gas separation processes to remove impurities.</li>
    <li><b>Nuclear Waste Management:</b> Utilized for efficient capture of radioactive ions and stabilization of nuclear waste into durable ceramic forms.</li>
    <li><b>Energy Storage & Commercial:</b> Used in solar thermal collectors, adsorption refrigeration, laundry detergent formulations, and as an additive in warm-mix asphalt.</li>
    <li><b>Medical & Household:</b> Used in oxygen concentrators for medical-grade oxygen and in household products for moisture/odor control and pet bedding.</li>
  </ul>
