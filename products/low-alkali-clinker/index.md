  <h1>Low-Alkali Clinker</h1>
  <p>Low-Alkali Clinker is produced under strict chemical constraints to limit the total content of sodium and potassium oxides, preventing the Alkali-Silica Reaction (ASR) which causes concrete expansion and degradation.</p>

  <h2>Key Advantages</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>ASR Mitigation:</b> Eliminates the risk of expansive reactions between cement and reactive aggregates.</li>
    <li><b>Structural Longevity:</b> Guarantees extended service life for high-stakes national projects.</li>
    <li><b>Environmental Compatibility:</b> Reliable performance with various aggregate types.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Primary Applications</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Strategic Infrastructure:</b> Highways, long-span bridges, and dams.</li>
    <li><b>High-Durability Projects:</b> Infrastructure designed for multi-decade service life.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Classification & Standards</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><b>ASTM Standard:</b> Compliant with ASTM C150 requirements (Optional alkali limitation).</li>
    <li><b>Quality Class:</b> Premium Low-Alkali Industrial Grade.</li>
    <li><b>Chemical Profile:</b> Strict limitation on Na2O (Total alkali content).</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Packaging & Logistics</h2>
